Who Are We?

At Collective Arts we mix our beers with art. We believe that art inspires our beer and hopefully our beer inspires your art. Every 3 months we change the art on our bottles thanks to our loyal fans, and its not just visual art we accept. We want to hear what you have to say, or play. On our cases we offer a special code on the back so you get to hear great music while you slip some delicous beer. How awesome is that?

We always cut through the competion!
